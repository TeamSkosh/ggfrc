About GGFRC

GGFRC (Greater Grand Forks Robotics Club) is an introductory hands-on robotics club. We plan to have bi-monthly meetings during the school year in which students will be exposed to and learn skills in regards to the LEGO® NXT robotics kit. In this club students can attend any meetings they please, and it will not require a commitment by the student. Attendance is optional, and there are no assignments. The GGFRC is a program in which students of all ages can come and learn about robotics and critical thinking in a positive and friendly environment. GGFRC has no age requirements.

Note: If you are bringing students younger than 2nd grade, you are required to stay with your student during the club meeting.

Who We Are

We are a non-profit, volunteer-led organization whose mission is to introduce students of all ages to robotics, creative processes, and critical thinking skills to help them get excited and passionate about S.T.E.M. (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math).

We strive to offer these programs at no cost to the student. We work diligently to find support in the forms of competent and responsible coaches, financial support, and offerings of adequate places for FIRST® LEGO® League teams to meet.

FIRST® LEGO® League

FLL (FIRST® LEGO® League) is a structured competition program that runs from the beginning of October to early February. It will require a significant commitment from the student to meet with their group and complete assignments every week. FLL is a program in which a student learns robotics, researching, presentation, teamwork and other important skills, both S.T.E.M. and life related.
