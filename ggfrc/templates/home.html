About GGFRC

FIRST® LEGO® League

It is that time again for Lego League! This years competition is Trash Trek. Sign-up Here!

FLL (First Lego League) is a structured competition program that runs from the beginning of October to early February. It will require a significant commitment from the student to meet with their group and complete assignments every week. FLL is a program in which a student learns robotics, researching, presentation, teamwork and other important skills, both S.T.E.M. and life related.

FFL requires that the student be at least 9 years old by the dates of the regional competition (February 6th, 2016) but not older than 14 years old at the start of the challenge release date (August 25th, 2015) . i.e. - Age Requirement: 9 - 14 While we would love to have every age-appropriate student participate in FLL we are limited by funding, coaches, and meeting space. This year we are offering 5 teams with a tentative maximum of 5 students per team (35 students total).

Unfortunately this means that your registration does not guarantee your student's placement on a team.

Students will be placed on teams through a variety of criteria such as: age, previous experience, parental involvement, time of registration, etc... The administrating body of the GGFRC, made up of coaches, will make the final decisions on the composition of the teams that provide the best opportunities for the students to learn, grow and succeed.
